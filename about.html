My Professional Journey

\

\ Hello! I'm Ashish Bhargav Gampa, a dedicated DevOps and Cloud Engineer with over five years of hands-on experience in transforming IT operations and infrastructure. My journey has been focused on bridging the gap between development and operations, ensuring rapid, reliable, and scalable software delivery. I'm passionate about leveraging cutting-edge cloud technologies and automation to build resilient and cost-efficient systems.\

\

\ My expertise spans across major cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, and GCP, where I've specialized in everything from infrastructure provisioning with IaC tools like Terraform and CloudFormation, to orchestrating microservices with Kubernetes. I thrive on creating robust CI/CD pipelines that automate deployments and ensure zero-downtime releases. I also have a strong commitment to cloud security best practices and continuous monitoring, ensuring robust and compliant environments.\

\

\ I believe in continuous learning and enjoy solving complex challenges that drive business efficiency and innovation. Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new tech gadgets and hiking.\
